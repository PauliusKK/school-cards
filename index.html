HERE IS WHY YOUR GAME IS BROKEN:

Your preflop game is pretty decent, you’re raising or calling at the right times, but then when the board comes you find yourself lost. You call continuation bets and you are comfortable bluffing on the flop or turn, but then the river comes and you don’t always know what to do.
